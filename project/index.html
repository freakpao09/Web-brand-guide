Our brand principles

Clarity in complexity

We translate algorithm shifts, AI search changes, and platform updates into plain-language reporting marketers can act on today.

Editorial authority

Since 2003. We report, we explain, we prescribe — backed by practitioners who run campaigns, not theorists who observe them.

Practical, never academic

Every piece ships with how-tos, frameworks, and screenshots. If it doesn't help a reader do their job Monday morning, it doesn't ship.

Sober, confident tone

No emoji, no hype, no hedging. Title Case headlines, declarative sentences, and numbers that have been checked twice.
